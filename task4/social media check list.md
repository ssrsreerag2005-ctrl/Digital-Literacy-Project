# Part B – Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist
| Things To Do                                                                 | Things Not To Do                                                                                      |
|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Use strong and unique passwords for all your accounts.                       | Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly.                                              |
| Think before you post — consider how others might perceive what you say.     | Avoid posting content that could be hurtful or unkind to others.                                      |
| Keep your privacy settings at a level that makes you feel safe.              | Never click on suspicious links or messages — they may be attempts to steal your information.         |
| Only share information that is true and from trustworthy sources.            | Avoid getting into arguments online — it is rarely productive and can be stressful.                   |
| Communicate professionally and respectfully, just as you would in person.   | Do not share your location or daily routine publicly, as this can be a safety risk for you and others.|
