# Part B – Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist
| ✅ Do’s | ❌ Don’ts |

|--------|----------|

| Use unique passwords for all accounts | Do not share personal or sensitive information on public platforms |

| Think before you post any content online | Avoid posting things that are offensive or not suitable |

| Keep your privacy settings up, to date | Do not click on links or messages that seem suspicious |

| Share information that is accurate and reliable | Avoid getting into arguments or bullying others online |

| Be respectful and professional when you communicate online | Do not share much about where you are or what you are doing |
