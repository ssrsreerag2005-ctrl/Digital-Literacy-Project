# Part B – Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist
| Things To Do                                                                 | Things Not To Do                                                                                      |

|------------------------------------------------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|

| Use unique passwords for all of your accounts.                       | Do not share your sensitive information publicly.                                              |

Think carefully before you post something online. Consider how other people might perceive what you say.     | Avoid posting content that could be hurtful or unkind to people.                                      |

Keep your privacy settings at a level that makes you feel safe and comfortable.              | Never click on links or messages. They may be attempts to steal your personal information.         |

| Only share information that's true and from sources that you trust.            | Avoid getting into arguments with people it is rarely productive and can be really stressful.                   |

| Communicate with people in a professional and respectful way just like you would when you talk to them in person.   | Do not share your location or daily routine publicly because this can be a safety risk for you and the people, around you.|
