# Case Study: MobiKwik UPI Payment Fraud (2025)

# Crime Type: Online Payment Fraud / UPI Fraud

* Description

Something bad happened to MobiKwik in September 2025. This Indian company that deals with money online had a problem with fraud. Some people found a way to cheat the system after the company updated its software. Over two days on September 11 and 12 these fraudsters took a lot of money about Rs 40 crore, which's around €4.3 million. They took this money from MobiKwiks accounts.

# Step-by-Step Execution

Exploitation of System Flaw

The fraudsters found a weakness in MobiKwiks system that let them make UPI payments when the wallet did not have enough money.

Bypassing Security

In cases the system let people use wrong UPI PINs, which is a big mistake.

Undetected Activity

No one noticed anything for two days until a routine check on September 13.

# Arrests

Later six people were. The investigators think it might have been an inside job because the fraud was very clever.

Targets and Consequences

MobiKwik was the victim but over 500,000 fake transactions affected many users. The consequences were:

* Financial Loss. Rs 40 crore was taken and only Rs 14 crore was recovered

* Regulatory Scrutiny. It showed that MobiKwiks systems to detect fraud were not good

* Reputational Damage. This was the big security problem for MobiKwik so people started to not trust the company

This case shows how not watching things closely and having threats from inside can lead to big online payment fraud.
