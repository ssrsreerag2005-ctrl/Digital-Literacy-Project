# Digital Literacy Quiz

This quiz is made using Google Form. It tests how well you understand important digital literacy concepts. Digital literacy concepts include safety and responsible internet usage and cybersecurity awareness and professional communication. The quiz has interactive questions that help you learn literacy concepts and it teaches you how to be safe and act properly in the digital world.
